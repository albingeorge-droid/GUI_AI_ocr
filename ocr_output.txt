Permission for grant of change of land use and composition
of unauthorized construction - M/s Papal Modi Ltd., Soniipat.

Reference : Your application dated 32.7.98 on the subject cited above.

Permission is hereby granted for change of land use after
regularization of illegal/ unauthorized construction and 2543 sq.mtrs.
construction of industrial building over an area
4246 sqyards. Situated in Khasra No. 92//1/4,2,3,4,7,8
Hirri/Basti at the revenue estate of Village Kansapur Distt.
The controlled area notified vide notification No. 11, 45, 5057
dated 11.5.1995.

This permission is further subject to the following
conditions:-

1) That the land of the premises is within the
jurisdiction of Urban Development Authority, Haryana
Chandigarh, and the provisions of Punjab Urban
Development Authority Act, 1973 and rules framed thereunder are
duly complied with by you.

2) That you shall provide employment to the persons
of Malaysia and/or local unemployed and unskilled
category was given due preference to Haryana to the Deptt.
of Town & Country Planning, Haryana, Chandigarh.

3) That you shall pay the outstanding amount of conversion
charges for any violation of land use or any lapse
within 30 days from the date of demand from the
Directorate, Town & Country Planning, Haryana, Chandigarh.

4) That the land shall be devoted to the permitted use
only if not more than 25% of the total/leased covered area is
used for other purposes also in
valid period of change of land use falling which N.O.C.
for the balance area shall have to be
obtained.

5) That this permission shall be valid for a period of two
years from the date of issue of this letter subject to
fulfillment of the terms and conditions of the permission
granted and agreement executed.

6) That this permission will not provide any immunity from
any other acts/rules/bldg. rules etc. applicable to the land
in question.
